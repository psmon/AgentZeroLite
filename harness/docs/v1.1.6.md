- **`harness/knowledge/wpf-xaml-resource-and-window-pitfalls.md`** ‚Äî Records
  five WPF traps that crashed AgentZero Lite during the

The five pitfalls:

| # | Pitfall | Repro |
|---|---|---|
| P1 | `<StackPanel.Resources>` style not visible from sibling `<Border>` | `MiniKeyButton` ‚Äî fix in `69f93a3` |

| P3 | `Window.Resources` does NOT inherit from `Owner` | new `TestToolsWindow` in `9606394` declared its own resources |

## Why this lands as knowledge, not as a one-shot fix

All five pitfalls passed `dotnet build` cleanly. The build-green / tests-
green signal is therefore *insufficient* for WPF/XAML diffs in this
codebase ‚Äî only running the app catches the crash. That makes a
checklist-style review the right intervention: code-coach already runs
Mode 2 before every commit; adding five greps to its workflow is
minutes, and it covers the entire class of "crash on first click into
new UI" failures.
